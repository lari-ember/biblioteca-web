Introducing Amber Archively - the program that will bring your personal library to life! Whether you're a bookworm, manga lover, or anime enthusiast, Amber Archively is the perfect platform to keep track of your personal collection. With features like user registration and personal library creation, you can easily organize your books and keep them at your fingertips. Add books with unique codes based on the CDU, and input details like title, author, publisher, year of publication, number of pages, and format (physical, PDF, or e-book). Want to find a specific book in your collection? No problem! Amber Archively allows you to search by any of the previously mentioned details, as well as by genre, reading status (currently reading, read, or to be read), and loan status (borrowed or owned).

But that's not all - with Amber Archively's filtering and classification system, you can easily sort your collection by author, publisher, year of publication, number of pages, and more. Keep track of which books you've read and mark your progress on those you're currently reading. And for all the statistics lovers out there, Amber Archively generates insightful information about your library, including the number of books per genre and your most-read author. So, go ahead and take a sip of your juice box while you peruse your personal library, and let Amber Archively do the rest!!
